Pollution!

Every minute there are one million tons of rubbish new produced! Every year ten million tons of that end in the oceans. This creates problems.

Firstly, lots of people buy a new plastic bag in the supermarket. When they are at home, they throw it away, straight up into the wilderness. The wind blows it into the forests and into the sea. When million tons of plastic garbage ends in the habitat of the animals, there are many problems, all living beings suffer from.
Secondly, if the plastic is burned in a garbage incinerator, the toxic gases will go up in the air and destroys the atmosphere. Then, the sunrays won't get filtered and come up directly on the Earth.
